CHURCH

Our MISSION

The supreme mission of the CHURCH,and also of every individual BELIEVER,is to glorify GOD and to serve Him forever. There for,if what we do as a CHURCH or as individual,cannot serve the glory of GOD,it shold not be done.

Our VISION

That every BELIEVER has the heart of a soul winner and is able to share the GOSPEL properly and bring them to maturity through DISCIPLESHIP

Our GOALS

Our goals are to serve GOD and bring GLORY to HIM. Certain GOD ordained goals are established in Scripture for the local CHURCH. These are basically set forth in the Great Commission.
